Will Gavin Rossdale have to pay $1 million to keep nanny Mindy Mann quiet?

September 2024 · 3 minute read

Earlier this week, E! News got their hands on some “exclusive photos” of Mindy Mann, Gavin Rossdale and Gwen Stefani’s former nanny and Gavin’s former lover/mistress of three years. Go here to see the photos. It was pretty obviously a set-up pap stroll (E! even had a videographer taping the whole thing), and it made people wonder if Mindy was going to try to Christine Ouzounian this situation. “To Ouzounian” = set up pap strolls, sell stories to the media, brag about your celebrity conquest on social media and walk away with a juicy, undercover payoff.

The Ouzounian comparison doesn’t exactly work in this case because we only found out about Mindy after Gwen and Gavin had finalized their divorce. Plus, Gavin has never tried to convince us that he’s some wholesome family man, which is what Ben Affleck tries to do on a regular basis. Still, the National Enquirer claims that Mindy might end up getting some money:

Gavin Rossdale might end up paying Mindy Mann $1 million in hush money to keep her from spilling the beans about their sex trysts.

Pals of Gavin are telling him the steep price is possible to “prevent the secrets from his reported extramarital affair from going public. Gavin is going to be made out to be the world’s worst husband if the nanny reveals al the sordid details.”

[From The National Enquirer, print edition]

$1 million to keep her quiet? Eh. Here’s the question: if she tried to sell her story, how much would she get? My guess is very low six figures. As I said, Gavin Rossdale is no Ben Affleck. Plus, Gavin and Mindy’s affair was reportedly going on for three years. At some point, feelings were involved. It wasn’t just sex on a Lear jet on the way to Vegas. Mindy might be staying quiet for now because she thinks she and Gavin might be real, that she’s going to end up being his official girlfriend.

Meanwhile, Us Weekly reports that Gwen Stefani and her children have been invited to Oklahoma for the Thanksgiving holiday!

Blake Shelton has invited his new girlfriend, Gwen Stefani, home for the holidays! A source tells Us Weekly that the country superstar, 39, invited Stefani and her three sons to spend Thanksgiving at his home in Oklahoma. It’s not yet known if she’s decided to take the country crooner and fellow Voice coach up on his offer, but Stefani is no stranger to Oklahoma. As previously reported by Us, Shelton took the No Doubt singer to his hometown earlier this month prior to her rep confirming exclusively to Us just ahead of the CMAs, that the two are dating.

[From Us Weekly]

What do you think? I think it’s way too early for Gwen to bring her kids to Oklahoma for an actual holiday. Like, with Blake’s extended family? No. It’s too soon. That being said, Adam Levine said that spending a week with Blake at Blake’s Oklahoma home was the best vacation he ever had. I think Blake has go-karts and tons of farm animals and a huge supply of liquor at his house. Which… I mean, that sounds like a really fun Thanksgiving.
