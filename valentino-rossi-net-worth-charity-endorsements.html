Legal Troubles

In 2007, Rossi was investigated by the Italian tax authorities for tax avoidance.

They investigated his undeclared revenues of $160 million from 2000 to 2004.

However, in February 2008, the case was closed after Rossi paid $35 million to the Italian tax authorities.

Valentino Rossi | Career

Valentino Rossi made his debut in 1996 from Grand Prix motorcycle racing.

Throughout his career, Rossi has won MotoGP World Championship seven times, 250cc World Championship one time, and 125cc World Championship one time.

Aside from motorcycle, he has also participated in car racing events including Gulf 12 Hours and GT World Challenge Europe.

Rossi has made 372 starts, has won 89 times, and has been to the podium 199 times in the MotoGP World Championship.

Similarly, he has covered the fastest laps 76 times, collecting a total of 5,415 points.

Furthermore, for the 250cc World Championship, he has made 30 starts winning 14 times, and has collected 510 points.
