Sibel Kekilli is a German actress. She started her acting career in the mid 2000s on German screen. Her debut was in the movie Head-On. Her first international project was in Fay Grim. She went on to star in the German movies The Last Train, Eve Dönüş and Pihalla. Her performance in the German-Turkish movie When We Leave earned her acclaim. In the 2010s, she became known internationally for playing Shae on Game of Thrones.
