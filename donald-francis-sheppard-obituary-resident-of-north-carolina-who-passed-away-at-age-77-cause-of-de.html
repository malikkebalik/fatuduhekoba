Family and Predeceased Relatives

Donald Francis Sheppard was preceded in death by his beloved siblings: Dorris Sheppard, Paul Sheppard, Brenda Sheppard, and Shelby Sheppard. He was also preceded by his parents, Paul Howard, Sr. and Edna Hargett Sheppard. Although they are no longer with us, their memory lives on in the hearts of those who loved them.

Donald is survived by his loving wife, Joyce Burris Sheppard, who stood by his side through thick and thin. He was a devoted father to his daughters, Kim Sheppard of Troy and Stephanie Hinson (Jamie) of Troy. Donald’s siblings, Ronald Sheppard (Pearl) of Star, Frank Sheppard (Lynn) of Mt. Airy, John Sheppard of Troy, and Nancy Allred (Donald) of Candor, were a source of strength and support throughout his life.

In addition to his immediate family, Donald leaves behind a network of extended family members who will miss him dearly. His sisters-in-law, Joyce B. Sheppard of Eagle Springs, Marie Sheppard of Troy, Linda Hunt of Troy, and Shirley Luther (Jim) of Troy, were cherished members of his life. He was also a proud grandfather to Danen Hinson, Delana H. Cagle, Grant Hinson, Brynn H. Bourgoin, and Luke Hinson. Furthermore, Donald was blessed with five great-grandchildren, as well as numerous nieces and nephews who brought joy to his life.

We encourage you to join us in sharing stories, memories, and offering support to his family during this time of remembrance.

Funeral Service and Burial

In remembrance of Donald’s life, a funeral service will take place at the Tabernacle Baptist Church on Friday, December 29th, 2023 at 2:00 PM. This service will be a time of reflection, celebration, and farewell as we gather to honor the memory of a remarkable individual. Following the funeral service, a burial will be held at the First Wesleyan Church Cemetery, located at 1368 NC-134, Troy, North Carolina 27371. This final resting place will serve as a peaceful sanctuary where Donald’s physical presence will find eternal rest.

During these solemn occasions, we invite all who knew and loved Donald to join us in bidding him a heartfelt farewell. Your presence and support will provide comfort to his family and friends as they navigate through this difficult time of loss and grief.
