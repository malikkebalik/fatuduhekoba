Park Ji-hoo Reveals the K-Drama Was Filmed While She Was in High School, Almost Two Years Ago

August 2024 · 3 minute read

TV

'All of Us Are Dead' stars rising actors who are in their early 20s with Park Ji-hoo still a high school student during filming. The cast of the K-drama congratulates Park as she is an official high school graduate.

Published on February 9, 2022

3 min read

A majority of the main cast for Netflix‘s All of Us Are Dead Korean drama are still relatively young. As rising actors, some of them are still in their early 20s. The cast’s young appearances made it easy for audiences to believe they were real students trapped inside their high school trying to survive. Fans will be stunned to learn All of Us Are Dead actor Park Ji-hoo was a high school student at the time the K-drama was filmed.

Park Ji-hoo plays the leading role of On-jo in ‘All of Us Are Dead’

The Netflix coming-of-age zombie K-drama focuses on a group of friends and students as their school becomes ground zero for a viral outbreak. One of the leading characters and unofficial narrators is On-jo, played by Park in All of Us Are Dead. On-jo is like any other girl who wants to confess to her longtime crush, raised by a single father, and has close friends.

In All of Us Are Dead, she becomes the group’s voice of reason when the Jonas virus, created by the school science teacher, turns their friends into monsters. Throughout the K-drama, On-jo becomes a favorite for her kind heart while fans have noticed a discrepancy in her story.

‘All of Us Are Dead’ was filmed two years ago when Park Ji-hoo was in high school

While All of Us Are Dead hit Netflix in January 2022, the K-drama was filmed years prior. According to Cosmopolitan, the cast and crew filmed the drama almost two years ago. Looking at Instagram posts from the actors, many behind-the-scenes photos are time-stamped for 2020.

According to details about the actors, All of Us Are Dead’s Park Ji-hoo is currently 18-years-old having been born in 2003. But by Korean age standards, she is closer to 20. If the K-drama was filmed in 2020, this means Park was roughly 16 or 17 at the time and a high school student just like her character.

In a cast interview for Netflix discussing what drew them to the K-drama, Park explained, “For me, as a high school student, a school is my second home, right? I think it’s very interesting as it’s a story happening in a school.” With Park still fresh to the experience of being a student, she added a level of realism to her role fans adored.

“I spent my two years of high school living as On-jo. So I’m glad I was able to relate to her in the way I did,” said Park. When discussing her relationship with the cast, she says they felt like her real school friends and the final day of filming felt like graduation.

The K-drama cast congratulates the actor on her high school graduation

As the youngest cast member of All of Us Are Dead, Park is officially a high school graduate. According to Soompi, the actor posted photos on Instagram to celebrate her graduation from Dongmoon High School. Thankfully, there were no zombies at Park’s school.

Park’s All of Us Are Dead co-stars posted heartfelt comments congratulating the actor on her graduation. Cho Yi-hyun, who plays Nam-ra, teased Park by commenting, “Wow… now you’re really an adult..” Meanwhile, Yoon Chan-young, who plays Park’s on-screen love interest, commented, “Congratulations on graduating, junior.”

According to Soompi, Yoon calls Park his “junior” for a reason. The recently graduated All of Us Are Dead actor will attend Hanyang University. Her co-star Yoon is currently a theatre and film major at the university.
