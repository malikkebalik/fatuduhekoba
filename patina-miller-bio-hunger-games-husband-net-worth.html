Early Life and Education

The versatile actress was born on November 6, 1984, in Pageland, South Carolina, the United States of America. Her full name is Patina Renea Miller. Also, she was raised by her mother, Robin Burch, in a single-parent home.

Similarly, Miller holds an American nationality and is of African-American ethnicity. As for her education, she studied Arts and Humanities at South Carolina Governor’s School. After receiving a full scholarship, Miller enrolled in Carnegie Mellon University and graduated in 2006.
