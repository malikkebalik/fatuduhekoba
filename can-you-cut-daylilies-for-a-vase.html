Can you cut daylilies for a vase?

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Daylilies are gorgeous flowers not often used in floral arrangements because of their reputation for being hard to work with. Using your sharp scissors or garden snips, cut 1 inch off the bottom of each daylily stem at a sharp angle.
