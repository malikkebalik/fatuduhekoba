Tagliafierro, 50, began her public-sector career as a legislative assistant to state Sen. Serphin Maltese, R-Queens. She then worked as an assistant district attorney in Erie County for eight years.

Tagliafierro was appointed director of intergovernmental affairs after Cuomo was elected governor.

She joined the newly formed state Joint Commission on Public Ethics, first as its director of investigations before rising to Executive director in 2013.
