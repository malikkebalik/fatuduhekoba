What Is the Original NCIS Casts Net Worth?

July 2024 · 4 minute read

NCIS has seen many characters come and go. However, fans will always love the original cast. Many would be happy to see some of them return to the show. Here’s how much the original NCIS cast is worth.

Mark Harmon

Net worth: $100 million

Mark Harmon made his acting debut in a 1973 episode of the television show Ozzie’s Girls titled “The Candidate.” After that, he appeared in a 1975 episode of the TV series Emergency!

Harmon rose to fame after starring in the medical drama St. Elsewhere. He played Dr. Robert Caldwell from 1983 to 1986. Other television appearances include Charlie Grace, Chicago Hope, and The West Wing.

In 1987, Harmon played Freddy Shoop in the movie Summer School opposite actress Kirstie Alley. He then starred in the 1988 military movie The Presidio alongside Sean Connery. Other film appearances include Stealing Home, Till There Was You, and Wyatt Earp. As of this writing, Harmon has an estimated net worth of $100 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Michael Weatherly

Net worth: $45 million

Michael Weatherly made his acting debut in a 1991 episode of The Cosby Show titled “Theo’s Final Final.” He played the role of Theo’s roommate. That same year, he appeared in an episode of the daytime drama Guiding Light. In 1992, Weatherly secured a recurring role on the daytime drama Loving. He played the character Cooper Alden for 15 episodes. Weatherly reprised his role as Cooper Alden in The City, a series that picks up where Loving leaves off. He now stars in the CBS series Bull, playing the role of Jason Bull. As of this writing, Weatherly has a net worth of $45 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth estimates.

Pauley Perrette

Net worth: $22 million

One of Perrette’s early roles was in a 1994 ABC Afterschool Special titled “Magical Make-Over.” In 1996, she landed a recurring role in the series Murder One. She played the character Gwen for 10 episodes from 1996 to 1997. Perrette made her film debut in the 1997 movie The Price of Kissing, in which she played Renee. In 1999, Perrette joined the cast of The Time of Your Life. She played the role of Cecilia Wiznarski for 19 episodes from 1999 to 2000. Some of Perrette’s other acting roles include appearances in Broke, Fantasy Hospital, NCIS: New Orleans. As of this writing, Perrette has an estimated net worth of $22 million according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Sasha Alexander

Net worth: $20 million

One of Sasha Alexander’s early roles was in the 1997 movie Visceral Matter, in which she played the role of Karen Chambers. Alexander landed her first recurring role in the television series Wasteland. She played Jesse Presser for 13 episodes in 1999. Her next recurring role was in Dawson’s Creek, in which she played Gretchen Witter from 2000 to 2001.

Her other acting roles include appearances in CSI: Crime Scene Investigation, Friends, and Mission: Impossible III. As of this writing, Alexander has an estimated net worth of $20 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

David McCallum

Net worth: $10 million

David McCallum made his acting debut in 1953 in the mini-series The Rose and the Ring. He played the role of Giglio. In 1957, he made his film debut in the movie Night Ambush. McCallum landed a recurring role in the TV series Our Mutual Friend, in which he played the role of Eugene Wrayburn from 1958 to 1959. He got his big break after playing Ashley-Pitt ‘Dispersal’ in the 1963 film The Great Escape.

McCallum is known for playing Illya Kuryakin in the series The Man from U.N.C.L.E. from 1964 to 1968. His other television appearances include The Replacements, The Education of Max Bickford, Kidnapped, Law & Order, and Sex and the City. As of this writing, McCallum has an estimated net worth of $10 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Sean Murray

Net worth: $8 million

One of Sean Murray’s early roles was in the 1991 TV movie Backfield in Motion. The following year, he appeared in one episode of the television series Civil Wars. Murray made his film debut in the 1993 film This Boy’s Life, in which he played the role of Jim Vorhees. His big break came that same year when he played Thackery in Hocus Pocus. Murray’s other acting roles include appearances in River of Rage: The Taking of Maggie Keene, Harts of the West, Silk Stalkings, and ER. As of this writing, Murray has an estimated net worth of $8 million, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Brian Dietzen and Cote de Pablo

You likely noticed Brian Dietzen and Cote de Pablo weren’t on the list. It’s important to note these actors were not part of the original NCIS cast. Before Dietzen joined the series, actor Pancho Demmings played Ducky’s assistant, Gerald Jackson. Cote de Pablo’s Ziva David was a replacement for Agent Kate Todd, played by actor Sasha Alexander.
