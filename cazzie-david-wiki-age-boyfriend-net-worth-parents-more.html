Early Life & Parents

The 5 feet 6 inches tall actress Cazzie David aka Cazzie Laurel David, was born on May 10, 1994, to her mother, Laurie David, and father, Larry David, in Boston City in, Massachusetts, where she grew up under the supervision of her talented father, American comedian Larry David, and mother, American activist Laurie David, but when she was 13 years old, her parents got divorced.

In her family, she is not a single actress because, from her biological parents, she has a little sister Romy David who is also an actress but has appeared only in “Hannah Montana” 2007 episode “My Best Friend’s Boyfriend.”

Being born in the family of a successful comedian, her entry into the acting world was absolutely sure, for which she attended Emerson College, where she earned a BA in writing for film and television in 2016.
