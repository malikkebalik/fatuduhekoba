What is Paul Greengrass' Net Worth?

Paul Greengrass is an English director, producer, screenwriter, and journalist who has a net worth of $20 million. Paul Greengrass was born in Cheam, Surrey, England in August 1955. He co-founded the organization Directors UK in 2007 and served as President until 2014. Greengrass directed the film Resurrected in 1989. He wrote and directed the TV series Open Fire, The One That Got Away, and The Fix. Greengrass directed the movie The Theory of Flight in 1988 and wrote and directed the film Bloody Sunday in 2002.

He wrote, directed, and produced the movie Omagh in 2004 and won a British Academy Television Award for Best Single Drama. He directed the movies The Bourne Supremacy in 2004, The Bourne Ultimatum in 2007, and wrote, directed, and produced the movie Jason Bourne in 2016. Greengrass wrote, directed, and produced the movie United 93 in 2006 and won the BAFTA Award for Best Direction. He produced and directed the film Green Zone in 2010 and directed the film Captain Phillips in 2013 which was nominated for two Academy Awards, a BAFTA Award, and a Golden Globe Award. Greengrass was also nominated for an Academy Award for United 93.
