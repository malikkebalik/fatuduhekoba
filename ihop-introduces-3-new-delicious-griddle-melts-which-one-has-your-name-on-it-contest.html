IHOP Introduces 3 New Delicious Griddle Melts: Which One Has Your Name On It? (CONTEST)

August 2024 · 2 minute read

Yummy! IHOP has just introduced 3 awesome new items to their menu: Three custom griddle melts sure to rock your socks off. IHOP is known for providing its customers with wonderful new breakfasts, and they’re continuing in that tradition by adding some rocking new pieces of deliciousness to their restaurants. The sandwiches are sure to become a fast favorite, and they’re large, hearty, and hand crafted. Plus, they’re served on artisan sourdough bread and made to order for each guest, any time of day. How bad can that be? No too shabby in our opinion.

If you run on over to your nearest IHOP, here’s what you can expect to find:

Spinach, Roasted Pepper & Cheese 
Sautéed spinach in a fluffy egg omelet topped with roasted red peppers, onions, melted Provolone, Parmesan and Pepper Jack cheeses on grilled artisan sourdough bread.  Served with your choice of hash browns, French fries or seasonal fruit.

Western
Smoked ham and sautéed peppers and onions in a classic omelet, topped with sliced ham, melted American cheese and served on grilled artisan sourdough bread.  Served with your choice of hash browns, French fries or seasonal fruit.

Ham & Egg Melt 
Sliced ham and melted Swiss and American cheeses over a fluffy egg omelet served on grilled artisan sourdough bread.  Served with your choice of hash browns, French fries or seasonal fruit.
