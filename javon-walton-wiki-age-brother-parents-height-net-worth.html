Meet Ashtray Euphoria Actor Javon Walton

  • 15 years old Actor Javon Walton (Birth Date: July 22, 2006) is professionally a young Boxer and Athlete who has won the five-time Georgia State Championship.
  • At first, very few people knew Javon, but today everyone is talking about him for his outstanding performance as “Ashtray” in both seasons of the Euphoria series.
  • Walton did not have any acting experience before appearing in the ” Euphoria” series.
  • His journey to come into the “Euphoria” series started when he came on Steve Harvey’s show, where Casting director Jennifer Venditti observed him, who asked him to give an audition for the role of Ashtray.

  • Walton made his debut appearance in “Euphoria Season 1” on June 16, 2019, who’s first introduced as Fezco’s informally adopted younger brother.
  • In Euphoria Season 1, His character Ashtray is around 11 years old and appears with Rue Bennett for the first time, but at that time, he did not get as much popularity as he is getting today after the end of Euphoria season 2.
  • The Season 2 finale of Euphoria concludes with Walton’s character Ashtray’s death, bringing the character’s journey to a dramatic and quiet close, although how much of it is true can only be decided by Director Sam Levinson.
  • Walton told “Variety,” “Ashtray is a badass child; he isn’t fooling around. I feel he has a chance of being around for Season 3,” which is why there is still hope for Ashtray to remain alive.
